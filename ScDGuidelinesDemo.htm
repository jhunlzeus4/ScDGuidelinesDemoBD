Section 1 - Introduction

Welcome to the Scientific Department! Here, you will conduct a variety of new and exciting tasks to serve the Foundation! Here, in the Scientific Department, we hold curiosity, exploration, and imagination as our core values. Having any one of these traits will surely help you to excel in our ranks.

Section 2 - Ranking Structure

As with every other department within the SCP Foundation, ScD follows and adheres to a command structure. Each rank comes with its own set of abilities, rewards, attributes, and of course, responsibilities. The further up the Chain of Command the rank is, the harder it will be to gain that rank. Especially as positions in ScD command may not exceed a cap limit.

Chief Researcher | Level-4 | Limit 2
Those possessing the rank of Chief Researcher serve the leadership duties of the department. They are closely involved in the High Command, directly looking over their work. The Chief Researcher has full power over the department, with proper consultation from the Overseer.
